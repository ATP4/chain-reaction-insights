    This is the first post created using the famous micro-blogging app called ‘Publii‘. I love the ease of use, GitHub support for hosting pages and best of all a native app available on Apple Silicon MacOS.

    This blog will be about three topics : AI, FinTech and Blockchain.

    I choose these three topics simply based on my interest (rather than following a trend or fad). As someone who does not code regularly, I have built basic apps previously. Starting with Visual Basic and C# a decade ago to Python based data wrangling apps in 2023. In 2025, I am building iOS and WebApps using AI-based tools like A0.Dev and Bolt.Dev.

    I hope this blog can be helpful for enthusiasts, hobbyists, students and professionals alike. My mind is filled with apps, links and startups (like directories) that help me day to day exploring stuff online. For example, I am going to bookmark a page like https://pagecord.com/ to start my email newsletter if i have sufficient information/data to share with others that is relatable and interesting.

    Good to see you here and let’s keep reading and exploring ! Ciao ~
